COVID CHAOS

#stayhome#savelives

    While staying at home, save lives in a chaotic scenario by targeting vaccines to uninfect the infected, constructing clinics to immunize and quarantine balls to control the spread of infection using your physics/snooker skills! Earn a 'clinic' and 'quarantining vaccine' after saving every 5 from dying. Game ends when you (biggest ball on screen) die.

SCORE = #saved - #dead

PS: Remember the color codes:)

   Click on vaccine icon, then click on screen and drag to aim vaccine at an infected ball to cure it temporarily. The length of arrow denotes it's speed.

  Click on clinic icon, then click on screen to construct clinic and immunize balls that enter it.
